How it flows. A visitor chats with the bot on a Hamzury site (or fills a HUB form) → submission lands in the leads table → CSO sees the lead in the live CSO portal → CSO sells and assigns to the right back office (Bizdoc, Scalar, Medialy). Medialy plans content and briefs Studio for visuals. Studio also takes standalone work directly from CSO. Business Dev runs the growth engine across all of it — campaigns, grants, affiliates. Yes — HUB form fills DO appear in the CSO workspace once submitted.
