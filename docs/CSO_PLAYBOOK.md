# CSO Playbook

Complete operational guide for the Chief Sales Officer at HAMZURY. Source-of-truth: `original files/PHASE4_CSO_BIZDEV/CSO/`. This used to live as a tab inside the CSO portal — moved here in 2026-04-30 cleanup so the bundle stays tight.

## Mission & ownership

CSO is the only client-facing role at Hamzury. Every lead enters through CSO, every closed client is brought by CSO, every commission is earned by CSO. Divisions deliver — CSO sells, qualifies, closes, and maintains the relationship for upsells and renewals.

Three CSO sub-roles inside the team:

- **Lead Handler** — adds leads to the sheet within 30 min, sends M1 (Welcome + 3 Qs) within 2 hrs.
- **Closer** — qualifies, calls, handles objections, asks for the close.
- **Coordinator** — sends forms, PDFs, invoices; tracks payments; hands off to division.

**Founder rule:** Only CSO contacts clients. Bizdoc, Scalar, Medialy, Hub ops portals do not message clients — ever. They deliver, update task status, and CSO communicates the update outward.

## BANT qualification

Use this on every lead before moving past Qualification stage.

### B — Budget

Question: *"Do you have a rough budget range in mind?"*

Floor by service: Bizdoc CAC ₦25k+ · Scalar website ₦300k+ · Medialy package A ₦50k+ · Hub Code Craft ₦300k.

If budget is below floor: route to a smaller package or to Hub Online Academy. Don't undercut floor pricing.

### A — Authority

Question: *"Are you the decision-maker, or do you speak to someone else first?"*

If NOT decision-maker: ask who is, get their contact, send proposal to both.

### N — Need

Question: *"What specific challenge are you trying to solve?"*

Follow-up: *"What happens if it stays unsolved for 6 months?"*

Genuine pain → fast close. Vague aspirations → push to diagnostic form (Path B).

### T — Timeline

Question: *"Ideal timeline to start?"*

- **Immediately / 1 month** → Path A (direct close)
- **1–3 months / 3+** → Path B (diagnostic form, nurture in calendar)

## 11-stage CSO flow

Every lead moves through these stages. Pipeline cards mirror this.

### Stage 1–3 · Triage

1. Lead enters — Lead Handler adds to sheet within 30 min.
2. First response — M1 (Welcome + 3 Qs) sent within 2 hrs.
3. Qualification — answers reviewed; pick Path A or Path B.

### Path A · Direct close

Path A is for clear, simple needs. Skip the diagnosis form.

- 4A. Assigned to Closer.
- 5A. Closer calls within 24h, presents solution.
- 7. Closer call → Won / Nurture / Lost.

### Path B · Diagnosis

Path B is for complex needs that require a custom solution.

- 4B. Coordinator sends Message 3 (form link) within 4 hrs.
- 5B. Wait for form. If no response in 24h, follow up.
- 6B. Coordinator builds custom PDF + sends Message 4 within 24h of form return.
- 7B. Coordinator schedules call (Message 5) → moves to Stage 7.

### Stage 8 · Closer call outcome

Closer asks: *"Ready to move forward?"*

- **YES** → Coordinator sends invoice + agreement (Message 6A) within 1 hour. Status = Onboarding.
- **NO/MAYBE** → Closer schedules follow-up in 3–7 days. Status = Nurture.

### Stage 9–10 · Close + handoff

- 9. Payment received → Coordinator notifies division + creates HMZ-C client file.
- 10. Division begins delivery. CSO maintains relationship for upsells + renewals.

## Objection handling — top 5

Every objection has a script. Don't argue — acknowledge, reframe, redirect.

### "It's too expensive"

- **Acknowledge:** *"That's a real concern — let me show you the breakdown."*
- **Reframe:** Cost vs cost-of-inaction. *"What does it cost you NOT to have this for the next 12 months?"*
- **Redirect:** Offer the next package down (Bizdoc B → A; Medialy C → B), OR offer the payment plan.

### "We need to think about it"

- **Acknowledge:** *"Of course — totally understand."*
- **Probe:** *"What specifically are you weighing? Is it the price, the timeline, or whether we're the right partner?"*
- **Redirect:** Address the specific concern. Set a follow-up time + day.

### "We already work with someone"

- **Acknowledge:** *"Smart — you've done the homework."*
- **Reframe:** *"Many of our clients had a previous provider. Two questions: what's working with them, and what's not?"*
- **Redirect:** If gaps exist, offer to plug ONE gap as a trial (small package). Don't try to displace the whole relationship up front.

### "I'll do it myself"

- **Acknowledge:** *"You absolutely could."*
- **Reframe:** *"What's your hourly rate? At {rate}/hr the {service} takes about {hours} hours of your time, vs ₦{price} for us to handle it."*
- **Redirect:** Offer a hybrid — we do the heavy lifting, they review. Frame as augmentation, not replacement.

### "Send me an email and I'll review"

- **Acknowledge:** *"Sure thing — what email is best?"*
- **Reframe:** *"I'll send the proposal now. To make sure I tailor it correctly, can I ask you ONE question first?"* (use BANT)
- **Redirect:** Get one piece of qualification info before they go quiet, then send the proposal AND book the follow-up call in the same message.

## Daily routine (Mon–Fri)

Same rhythm every day. Boring is good — boring is repeatable.

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 8:30 | Check pipeline + plan day. Sort by stage age. Anything in New, Qualified, or Negotiation older than 3 days = red, action today. Note the top 3 priorities. |
| 9:00–11:00 | **Outreach block.** Hard block. No meetings. No admin. Calls + LinkedIn DMs + email follow-ups. Goal: 10–15 connects per day. This is where revenue is born. |
| 11:00 | Inbox triage. Process every reply that came in during the outreach block. Send M1 to anything new. Move qualified leads to Closer. |
| 12:00–1:00 | Lunch + email batch. Eat. Then a 20-minute email batch — never throughout the day. Email is async; treat it like one. |
| 1:00–3:00 | Calls + proposals. Closer call slots. Discovery, follow-up, closing. Coordinator builds proposals based on morning's qualified leads. |
| 3:00 | **Dashboard update.** Mandatory. Every lead's stage, every commission row, every targets row updated by 3:30pm. End-of-day handoff to Coordinator. |

## Targets & metrics

What CSO is measured on. The 18% commission depends on these.

### Monthly targets

- New leads: 20+
- Qualified: 15+
- Closed deals: 5+
- Revenue brought: ₦2M+
- Conversion rate (qualified → won): 25%+
- Pipeline value at any time: ₦5M+

### Commission structure

- 18% of every Naira CSO brings in.
- Calculated by Finance. Paid 1st of every month.
- Revenue from auto-renewals doesn't count — only NEW deals or NEW upsells.
- Lost deals get zero. Nurtured deals that close later still earn the 18%.

### Escalation triggers

- **Escalate to CEO** if: budget > ₦500k, scope is cross-division, legal threat from client, payment overdue > 14 days.
- **Escalate to Founder** if: brand/reputation issue, ethical question, or any decision affecting strategy.
